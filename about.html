Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Ahmed Siddiqui, Zia Strings began with a clear purpose: to fill a gap in the market for thobes that honor tradition without sacrificing comfort. Ahmed saw the need for garments that combined timeless craftsmanship with modern wearability â€” and set out to deliver just that.

Whether you're dressing for Jummah, Eid, or everyday excellence, we believe your thobe should reflect both tradition and quality.
