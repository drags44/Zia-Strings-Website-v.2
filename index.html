About Zia Strings

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Zia Strings began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, comfortable thobes that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern comfort. Our founder, Ahmed Al-Mansoori, noticed a gap in the market for premium thobes that didn't compromise on quality or comfort.

Today, we source only the finest fabrics and work with skilled artisans to create thobes that are perfect for everyday wear, special occasions, and everything in between.
